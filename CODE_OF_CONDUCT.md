# DJConnect Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

DJConnect is built for people who enjoy making useful, playful technology
together. We want the project community to be welcoming, respectful and focused
on good-faith collaboration.

Everyone participating in this repository is expected to help keep it a safe
and constructive place, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity,
gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal
appearance, race, religion, sexual identity and orientation, or technology
preferences.

## Expected Behavior

Examples of behavior that helps the community:

- Be respectful and kind in issues, pull requests, reviews and discussions.
- Assume good intent, while still being clear about bugs, risks and tradeoffs.
- Give and receive technical feedback without personal attacks.
- Keep discussions on topic and useful for future readers.
- Credit other people's work and respect licenses, trademarks and project
  boundaries.
- Avoid sharing secrets, private URLs, tokens, logs or screenshots that expose
  personal or system-sensitive information.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Examples of behavior that is not acceptable:

- Harassment, intimidation, stalking or sustained disruption.
- Insults, slurs, demeaning comments or personal attacks.
- Sexualized language or imagery in project spaces.
- Publishing someone else's private information without explicit permission.
- Deliberately introducing malicious code, credential leaks or unsafe
  instructions.
- Repeatedly ignoring maintainer guidance about scope, safety, licensing or
  community standards.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit or reject comments, commits, code, issues,
pull requests or other contributions that do not align with this Code of
Conduct. Maintainers may also limit or remove participation for behavior that
is harmful to the project or its community.

When possible, maintainers will aim for proportionate, transparent action:
clarifying expectations, asking for edits, closing unproductive discussions or
blocking participation when needed to protect contributors.

## Reporting

If you experience or notice behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, contact
the project maintainer through a private channel available on the maintainer's
GitHub profile or project contact page. Include relevant links, screenshots or
context if you can do so safely.

Reports will be handled with care. Maintainers will avoid sharing reporter
identity or sensitive details beyond what is necessary to review and respond to
the situation.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in this repository and other DJConnect project
spaces where community members interact around the project. It also applies
when someone's conduct outside project spaces directly affects the safety or
wellbeing of project participants.

## Attribution
